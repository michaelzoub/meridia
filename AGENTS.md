## Visual design

**NO GLOWING DESIGN** — Do not add glow, neon halos, or heavy luminous shadows on UI (no `shadow-[0_0_*]`, large `drop-shadow`, `blur-*` “blooms,” or pulse/glow keyframes for chrome). Use flat or near-flat treatments: borders, 1px rules, restrained solid or linear gradients, and clear typography contrast.
